Early Career

Lionel messi's career started with Barcelona. He started at the age of 16 but he had trained with Barca since he was around 14. He went in the record books on May 1, 2005 at the youngest player to score a goal for Barcelona. He also led Argentina to the title in the under-20 World Cup. With a shprt stature, speed, and relentless attacking stlye he was compared to another famous football player : Diego Maradona. Messi took Barcelona to success most noticably in 2009. Barca won the Champions League, La Liga, and Spanish Cup Super titles. Two years later he took home his first world player of th year more commonly known as the Balon d'Or. Year after year he kept on leading Barcelona to more Cups and victories.

Records broken

In 2012 Messi went on a record breaking year. He became the first player to score five goals in a Champions League. A few weeks later he surpassed Cesar Rodriguez's club-record of 232 goals and became Barclona's all time leading scorer. By the end of the year Messi had 91 goals and passed the record for 85 goals in a year. After he broke that record he was named the winner of the FIFA Balon d'Or for the fourth time in January or 2013. After a hamstring injury he broke another record and became the top scorer in La Liga and Champions League. After helping Barca achieve a hstoric second treble in 2015 he was given a fifth FIFA Balon d'Or.
