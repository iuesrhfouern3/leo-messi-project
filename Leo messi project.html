Player bio

Lionel Messi is commonly called the best soccer player in the world. He playes for the international squad for Argentina and also for the Spanish club FC Barcelona. He is currently 29 years old and is the top scorer in FC Barcelona. Lionel Messi as born in Rosario, Argentina. When he was young he experienced a hormonial problem in which the medical treatments were expensive. He moved to Spain with his parents and he tried out for Barcelona. When the coaches saw hat he could do, they signed him to the club and payed for his medical treatments.

He quickly grew with Barca and in 2004 he made his first deput against Espanyol and since he was adopted as one of their greastest idols. He won his first championship with la Liga in the season or 2004-05. Since then he has won every major club trophy in Camp Nou. in 2008-09 he had a stunning six championships. He has also claimed the Ballon d'Or five times throughout his carrer. On march of 2012 at 24 years old he became the all-time top scorer in Barcelona with over 232 goals. His hat trick against Real Madrid in 2014 also let him become the top scorer in El Clasico. Now in 2016 Lionel Messi has 17 or more awards including the 5 Balon d'Ors. He also has the mens olympic football tournament.
